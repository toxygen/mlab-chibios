Pinout of STM32F103RBT6 to the peripherals.

I2C connection
=========================================
PB6		SCL
PB7		SDA

PWM output
=========================================
PC6		Motor channel 1
PC7		Motor channel 2
PC8		Motor channel 3
PC9		Motor channel 4

UART connection to the EM-411 GPS module
=========================================
PA2		UART TX
PA3		UART RX

SPI output to the NRF24L01 radio module
=========================================
PA5		SPI SCK
PA6		SPI MISO
PA7		SPI MOSI
I/O PORTS
PC4		IRQ 
PC5		CE  (OUTPUT to change send/receive mode.)
